About Astavinayak Nursing Home

Founded in 2005, Astavinayak Nursing Home is dedicated to providing the highest quality healthcare in Asansol. We specialize in a range of services, from emergency care to advanced treatments in orthopedics, heart, and neurological care. Our experienced team is committed to ensuring each patient receives personalized and compassionate care.

At Astavinayak Nursing Home, we believe in making healthcare accessible to everyone. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of professionals, we are equipped to handle complex medical conditions and emergencies.
